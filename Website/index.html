Our Services

Classic Craftsmanship, Modern Precision

Every service is performed with the meticulous attention to detail and old-school technique that our members have come to expect.

Classic Haircuts

Traditional cuts executed with precision clippers and shears. From fades to tapers, pompadours to crew cuts — the foundation of our craft.

Straight Razor Shaves

Experience the luxury of a traditional straight razor shave with hot lather, warm towels, and a finish so smooth it speaks for itself.

Beard Shaping & Trimming

Sculpted beard lines and detailed trimming to complement your face shape. We turn a good beard into a great one.

Hot Towel Treatments

Relax into the warmth of our signature hot towel treatment. Opens pores, soothes skin, and elevates every visit to an experience.

Scissor Cuts

For the man who prefers a textured, natural look. Our scissor-over-comb technique delivers volume, flow, and timeless style.

Lineups & Edge-Ups

Crisp, clean lines that frame your face perfectly. Walk out with razor-sharp edges that keep you looking fresh all week.

We also offer Kids Haircuts and Hair Styling & Grooming services. Every member of the family is welcome at the shop.

Our Story

More Than a Haircut — It's a Brotherhood

The Old-School Way

Members Traditional Barbershop was built on a simple belief: a barbershop should feel like coming home. We honor the traditions of the craft — hot lather, straight razors, and real conversation.

Craftsmanship First

Every barber on our team is a trained craftsman, not just a hair cutter. We take the time to understand your style, your hair, and what makes you feel your best. That's why our members stay.

Community & Belonging

When you walk through our doors, you become a member — not just a customer. From regulars who've been coming for years to first-timers discovering us through word-of-mouth, everyone belongs here.

San Fernando Valley's Finest

Located in Mission Hills, we proudly serve Chatsworth, Sylmar, Granada Hills, and the entire San Fernando Valley. Our 4.9-star reputation is built one cut at a time.

Tradition. Craft. Loyalty.
